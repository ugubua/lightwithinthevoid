About Michael

I'm Michael Perry—coach, creator, and co-host of the New Ashla Podcast. My work blends psychology, philosophy, and grounded spirituality. After walking through grief, trauma bonds, and a hard reset on life, I help people who are in the dark find their own light—without spiritual bypassing or empty platitudes.
